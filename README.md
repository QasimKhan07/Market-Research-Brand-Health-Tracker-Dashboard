Overview:
Power BI dashboard to track and analyze brand health of EBM biscuit brands using market research survey data. The dashboard supports business decision-making by monitoring awareness, usage, brand imagery, and advertising performance across key consumer segments.

Key Objectives:
Track brand health over time
Compare performance across EBM brands
Monitor awareness-to-usage funnel
Analyze demographics and regional performance
Evaluate advertising effectiveness

Key KPIs:
Top of Mind (TOM)
Spontaneous Awareness
Aided Awareness
Total Awareness
Consideration
Past 12 Month Usage
Past 1 Month Usage
Past 1 Week Usage
Most Often Use

Data & Modeling:
Survey data (Excel/CSV)
Data cleaning with Python / Power Query
Custom DAX measures
Weighted analysis
Handling of missing values

Executive Summary

Demographic & Regional Analysis
